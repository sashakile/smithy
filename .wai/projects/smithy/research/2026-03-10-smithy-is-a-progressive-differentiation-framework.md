Smithy is a Progressive Differentiation Framework for Adaptive Agent Architecture. Core insight: AI agent decisions start at P4 (full LLM, ~$0.50/call) and are progressively differentiated toward P1 (Drools rules, ~$0/call, <1ms) as traffic patterns stabilize. Three deliverables: mr CLI, DR (Differentiation Runtime in Clojure), and the Progressive Differentiation Model (grounded in cell biology). Key cost reductions: ticket classification 94.5%, fraud detection 97.8%. v2.1 type system: all types have ≤7 required fields. 9 open architectural decisions in openspec/changes/ (DQ-2 through CG-9). Specs in docs/spec.md and docs/type-system.md.
