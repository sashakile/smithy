## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: PROP-008 [Priority: P2]: Evidence captures quantitative justification
The system SHALL record Evidence with a Proposal including: traces analyzed, coverage rate,
agreement rate, shadow results (strategy, sample-rate, agreement, p-value, latency comparison,
cost comparison), and cost projection. Cost projection SHALL distinguish gross savings from net
savings by recording the estimated shadow cost, trace-storage cost, review labor cost, and any
incremental runtime overhead required by the proposed change. Evidence SHALL include a payback
period derived from those overhead terms. A Proposal that claims economic improvement SHALL use
net savings, not gross savings, as the promotion metric.

#### Scenario: Evidence includes shadow agreement rate
- **WHEN** a Proposal completes shadowing
- **THEN** Evidence.shadow.agreement is populated with the measured agreement rate between current and proposed potency

#### Scenario: Evidence includes cost projection
- **WHEN** a Proposal is generated by mr plan
- **THEN** Evidence.cost-projection includes current-daily, projected-daily, gross-savings-pct, net-savings-pct, overhead-breakdown, and payback-days

### Requirement: PROP-010 [Priority: P1]: Advanced proposal kinds require baseline proof
The system SHALL block topology proposal creation and approval for a component lineage until that
lineage has established baseline proof. Baseline proof SHALL require at least one committed,
non-emergency differentiation in the lineage, a completed 14-day post-commit observation window,
compatible telemetry that satisfies the minimum sample requirement, excluded telemetry no greater
than the configured exclusion cap for the evaluated metric, no unresolved severity `:fatal`
regressions during that window, and positive net savings after measured framework overhead. A
baseline-proof evaluation SHALL record the included sample count, excluded sample count, excluded
percentage, and exclusion reasons. If baseline proof is absent, any attempt to create or approve a
topology proposal for that lineage SHALL fail with a blocking Fault that identifies the missing
proof conditions and the evidence shortfall.

#### Scenario: Topology proposal is blocked without baseline proof
- **WHEN** a split or fuse proposal is created for a lineage that has no committed standard differentiation with proven net savings
- **THEN** proposal creation fails with a blocking Fault describing the unmet baseline-proof conditions

#### Scenario: Topology proposal is allowed after baseline proof
- **WHEN** a lineage has a committed non-emergency differentiation, a completed observation window, and positive net savings after overhead
- **THEN** topology proposal creation may proceed subject to the remaining topology lifecycle rules

#### Scenario: Baseline proof fails when telemetry exclusions exceed the cap
- **WHEN** the observation window contains positive net savings but too many traces are excluded due to incompatible or unknown producer contracts
- **THEN** baseline proof fails with evidence showing the exclusion percentage and the excluded telemetry reasons
